django-versionutils¶

django-versionutils is a collection of reusable Django apps designed to provide everything you need to do robust, smart and extendable versioning, diffing and merging when working with Django.

The django-versionutils project was initially developed for the LocalWiki project. We wanted a way to abstract out the often tedious versioning-related aspects of writing a wiki system. When looking at all the existing versioning systems for Django, we found none suited our needs.
